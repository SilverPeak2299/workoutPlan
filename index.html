Your Weekly Workout Plan

Day 1 (Sunday): Lower Body Strength + Core

Warm-Up

  • Light Cardio (Jog or Jump Rope): 2-3 mins
  • Leg Swings: 10-12 swings each leg, each direction
  • Walking Lunges with Twist: 10 steps per leg
  • High Knees: 30 seconds
  • Bodyweight Squats: 10-12 reps

Workout

  • Back Squats - 4 x 8 reps
  • Lateral Lunges (Weighted) - 3 x 10 per side
  • Romanian Deadlift - 3 x 10 reps
  • Nordic Curls - 3 x 6-8 reps
  • Russian Twists - 3 x 15 per side
  • Plank Variations (Regular & Side Plank) - 3 x 45 seconds per position

Day 2 (Monday): Recovery Stretching Routine

Warm-Up

  • Light Cardio: 1 minute
  • Leg Swings: 10 per leg
  • Arm Circles: 10 forward, 10 backward

Stretching Routine

  • Hamstring Stretch - 1-2 mins per leg
  • Hip Flexor Stretch - 1 min per side
  • Glute Stretch - 1-2 mins per side
  • Quadriceps Stretch - 1 min per leg
  • Inner Thigh Stretch - 1-2 mins
  • Calf Stretch - 1 min per side
  • Spinal Twist - 1 min per side
  • Upper Back & Shoulder Stretch - 1-2 mins
  • Lat Stretch - 1 min per side

Day 3 (Tuesday): Upper Body Strength + Core

Warm-Up

  • Jump Rope or Light Cardio: 2-3 mins
  • Arm Circles and Shoulder Rolls
  • Scapular Pull-Ups: 10 reps

Workout

  • Pull-Ups - 3 x 8 reps
  • Dumbbell Bench Press - 4 x 10 reps
  • Ring Rows - 3 x 10 reps
  • Push-Ups - 4 x 12 reps
  • YTW Raises - 3 x 12 reps per position
  • Russian Twists - 3 x 15 per side
